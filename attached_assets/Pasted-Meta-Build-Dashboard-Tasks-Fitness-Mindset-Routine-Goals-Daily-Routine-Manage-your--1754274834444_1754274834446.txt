Skincare & Health Tips
🥗 Foods for Glowing Skin & Strong Hair
Vitamin C: Orange, Lemon, Amla, Papaya, Tomato
Carotene: Carrots, Spinach, Sweet Potatoes
Collagen: Almonds, Walnuts, Coconut Water, Cucumber
Iron & Biotin: Spinach, Almonds, Walnuts, Eggs
Hydration: 3-4 liters of water daily
💪 Daily Hair Drink: Amla Juice or Coconut Water every morning

❌ Avoid These
• Too much sugar & junk food (causes dull skin & hair fall)

• Oily & fried food (clogs pores & weakens hair roots)

• Excess caffeine - Switch to Green Tea instead

🔥 Expected Results: 2-4 weeks for glowing skin, no tan, fresh smell, thick hair
